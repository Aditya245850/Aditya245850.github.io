Auto-Forecast

As a fellow frugal shopper, I find myself constantly comparing prices of products at stores to those online to know whether or not I got a good deal or not. In particular, one of those products that I tend to focus the most on are cars. I want the best deal possible! I religiously help my friends and family when buying used cars that have been on the market for quite a bit to ensure that they get the best deal possible, yet I spent a lot of time on google trying to find the average price of the specifc car on the market...

So I built Auto-Forecast, using Random Forest, a machine learning algorithm used for linear regression, and trained it to predict the price of the used car using features: Name, Location, Year, Kilometers Driven, Fuel Type, Transmission, Owner Type, Mileage, Engine, Power, Seats and Price when bought new.

You can easily access it by pulling from docker hub with this command: docker pull adityapaluri799/auto-forecast
